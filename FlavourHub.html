<....Menu.html...> Menu

Appetizers:

Bruschetta al Pomodoro: Toasted bread topped with fresh diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and extra virgin olive oil.
Chicken Satay: Grilled skewers of marinated chicken served with peanut sauce and cucumber relish.
Vegetable Spring Rolls: Crispy spring rolls filled with mixed vegetables, served with sweet chili sauce.
Caprese Salad: Slices of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Soups and Salads:

Tom Yum Soup: Spicy and sour Thai soup with shrimp, mushrooms, lemongrass, and lime leaves.
Caesar Salad: Crisp romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing.
Miso Soup: Traditional Japanese soup with tofu, seaweed, and green onions.
Greek Salad: Fresh cucumbers, tomatoes, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese tossed in Greek vinaigrette.

Main Courses - Italian:

Spaghetti Carbonara: Pasta tossed with crispy pancetta, egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
Margherita Pizza: Classic pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves.
Chicken Alfredo: Grilled chicken breast served over fettuccine pasta in creamy Alfredo sauce.
Eggplant Parmesan: Breaded and fried eggplant slices layered with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Main Courses - Asian:

Chicken Pad Thai: Stir-fried rice noodles with chicken, tofu, bean sprouts, peanuts, and tamarind sauce.
Beef Bulgogi: Thinly sliced beef marinated in soy sauce, garlic, and sesame oil, served with rice and kimchi.
Vegetable Fried Rice: Jasmine rice stir-fried with mixed vegetables, eggs, and soy sauce.
Green Curry Chicken: Thai green curry with chicken, bamboo shoots, bell peppers, and Thai basil, served with steamed rice.

Desserts:

Tiramisu: Classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese.
Mango Sticky Rice: Sweet sticky rice topped with fresh mango slices and coconut milk.
Chocolate Lava Cake: Warm chocolate cake with a molten chocolate center, served with vanilla ice cream.
Cheesecake: Creamy New York-style cheesecake topped with your choice of fruit compote.

Beverages:

Thai Iced Tea: Sweetened Thai tea served over ice with a splash of evaporated milk.
Lemonade: Refreshing lemonade made with freshly squeezed lemons and a hint of sweetness.
Cappuccino: Espresso topped with frothed milk and a dusting of cocoa powder.
Mojito: Classic Cuban cocktail made with rum, fresh mint, lime juice, sugar, and soda water.
